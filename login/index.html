PartnerSync

PartnerSync is a project matching tool that connects Algonquin faculty, students and external partners. It is used to collaborate, submit project requests, and explore partnership opportunities. This platform bridges the gap between academia and the community, fostering innovative applied research projects that solve real-world challenges.

Create an Account

Sign up by identifying yourself as a Algonquin Faculty Member or Industry Partner to tailor your experience.

Submit a Request

Provide details about your project idea or area of interest to get started.

Explore Collaboration Opportunities

Browse through partner organizations or courses at Algonquin College to find your ideal collaborators and start working together!
